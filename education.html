💪

🧪

✅ Who Can Donate?

✅   Age 18–65 years
✅   Weight above 45 kg
✅   Haemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL
✅   90 days since last donation
❌   Fever or cold recently
❌   Pregnant or breastfeeding
❌   On certain medications
❌   Recent tattoo or piercing

🔍 Myths vs Facts

Donating blood is painful and dangerous
It's safe with just a small needle prick. Body replenishes blood within 24–48 hours.
You can get infected by donating blood
All equipment is sterile and disposable. Zero risk of infection at certified blood banks.
Donating blood makes you weak for weeks
Fluid replaces in 24 hrs, red cells in 4–6 weeks. Most donors feel normal within hours.
